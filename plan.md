## 🚗 Inventory Cockpit

### The Problem

Dealers are losing margin every single day on aging inventory — but they can't see it happening. There's no single place that shows what's at risk, what to do about it, and what the dollar impact of each action would be.

### What We Delivered

**See the big picture instantly**
> Total capital locked, how fast it's burning, and how you compare to the market — all at a glance. A color-coded heatmap shows your entire inventory split across four health stages.

**Every vehicle gets a health stage**

| 🟢 Fresh | 🟡 Watch | 🟠 Risk | 🔴 Critical |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0–15 days | 16–30 days | 31–50 days | 51+ days |
| Healthy margin | Starting to age | Margin eroding fast | Losing money daily |

**Know exactly what to do — ranked by dollar impact**
> The opportunity queue tells you: "Activate a campaign on this 2023 Tahoe to protect $2,400 in margin." Biggest wins always at the top.

**Deep dive into any vehicle**
> Tap a vehicle to see everything: how fast its margin is depleting, a countdown to break-even, lead performance, and a full timeline of what's happened since it hit the lot.

**See the future before it happens**
> "If Sold Today" shows what you'd net right now. "What If Nothing" shows what you'd lose in 7, 14, and 30 days. No more guessing.

**Take action without leaving the screen**
> Activate campaigns in three tiers (Standard, Accelerated, Maximum) with an instant preview of expected lead lift and margin saved. Upgrade from AI clone photos to real media with a guided step-by-step flow.

**Understand your financial exposure**
> See total capital at risk broken down by where vehicles came from (auction, trade-in, wholesale, direct) and by price band.

**Website intelligence**
> A 0–100 website health score, a conversion funnel showing where visitors drop off, and an estimate of how much revenue you're missing from low-converting vehicle pages.

**Just ask**
> An AI chat where dealers can type questions like "Which vehicles need attention?" and get instant, plain-language answers.

### Screens at a Glance

> **Dashboard** — The home base. Capital KPIs, aging heatmap, priority actions, vehicle table.
>
> **Vehicle Detail** — Everything about one vehicle. Margin meter, performance, projections, timeline, and action buttons.
>
> **Acceleration** — Campaign performance and ROI. Which campaigns are working, which vehicles should get one next.
>
> **Media Intel** — AI clone photos vs real photos, side by side. Which vehicles are waiting for an upgrade.
>
> **Capital Exposure** — Financial risk view. Where is the most money at stake.
>
> **Action Queue** — The to-do list, ranked by dollar impact. Filter by urgency, take action inline.
>
> **Website Score** — How well the dealer's site converts. Where leads are leaking.
>
> **AI Chat** — Natural language Q&A about the inventory.

## 📊 ROI Dashboard

### The Problem

Dealers and GMs have no way to draw a clear line from "Spyne AI answered a call" to "that became a closed deal worth $X." Without that visibility, it's hard to justify the investment or know where to optimize.

### What We Delivered

**One number that matters**
> Total revenue from AI-delivered leads, front and center, with month-over-month change.

**A simple story anyone can follow**

> 📞 Calls Handled → 🎯 Leads Delivered → 📅 Appointments Set → 🤝 Deals Closed

**Spotlight on missed opportunities**
> The dashboard flags revenue the dealer is leaving on the table — low show rates, after-hours volume that could be captured, missed callback follow-ups.

**Trends over time**
> Charts showing whether qualified calls, appointments, and revenue are trending up or down.

**How each AI agent is performing**
> Four AI agents (Sales Inbound, Sales Outbound, Service Inbound, Service Outbound) — each with its own calls handled, qualification rate, and customer ratings.

**Sales vs Service, side by side**
> Compare the two departments at every stage of the pipeline, from first call to closed deal.

**Quality health check**
> At-a-glance status on qualification rate, handle time, customer satisfaction, and transfer success — color-coded green/amber/red.

## 🔌 Spyne Flip Analytics

### The Problem

Sales reps walk into a demo without knowing if Flip will actually work on the dealer's website. They waste time troubleshooting — or worse, the demo fails in front of the prospect.

### What We Delivered

**Instant compatibility check**
> Every dealer is rated: ✅ Flip Ready, ⚠️ Partial Support (with the reason), or 🚫 Console Only (with a fallback link). Search by dealer name, filter by status.

**One-click demo launch**
> "Launch Flip Demo" takes you straight to the live demo on the dealer's site. "Guaranteed Demo" opens the console fallback — works 100% of the time.

**Sales performance visibility**
> Leaderboard showing who's running the most demos, plugin usage stats, success rates, and where things are breaking.

**12 views covering everything**
> Overview · Demo Feasibility · Leaderboard · Demo Funnel · Dealers · Coverage · Usage · VINI · Errors · Trends · Health · Export

# User Flows

> How real people will use each part of the product — written from their perspective.

---

## 🚗 Inventory Flows

---

### 1. Morning Check-In

*"I just opened the dashboard. What needs my attention today?"*

> **Who**: Dealer or Manager, start of day
>
> ① Land on the Inventory Cockpit
> → See three numbers immediately: total capital locked, daily burn, velocity score
>
> ② Glance at the aging heatmap
> → Spot how many vehicles are Fresh, Watch, Risk, or Critical
>
> ③ Check the priority sidebar
> → Today's most urgent vehicles: critical margin, near break-even, high buyer interest
>
> ④ Scan the opportunity queue
> → Actions ranked by dollar impact — biggest wins at the top
>
> ⑤ Act without leaving the page
> → Activate a campaign or upgrade media right from the dashboard

---

### 2. Deep Dive on a Vehicle

*"This 2023 Tahoe has been sitting 40 days. What's going on?"*

> **Who**: Dealer investigating a specific vehicle
>
> ① Search or click the vehicle from the dashboard
>
> ② See its health at a glance
> → Margin meter showing depletion, countdown to break-even, daily burn rate
>
> ③ Check how it's performing
> → Leads, appointments, clicks — and how clone photos compare to real ones
>
> ④ See projections
> → "If Sold Today" → what you'd net
> → "What If Nothing" → what you'd lose in 7, 14, 30 days
>
> ⑤ Review the full timeline
> → When the VIN came in, first lead, any media upgrades, stage transitions
>
> ⑥ Take action
> → Activate a campaign (pick a tier, see expected lift) or upgrade media (guided steps)

---

### 3. Boosting a Vehicle

*"I want to push this vehicle. What are my options?"*

> **Who**: Dealer activating a campaign
>
> ① Hit "Activate Campaign" — available from the dashboard, queue, or vehicle detail
>
> ② Pick a tier
> → Standard / Accelerated / Maximum
>
> ③ Preview the expected impact
> → Lead lift %, margin protected, days saved
>
> ④ Confirm — campaign goes live
>
> ⑤ Track results on the Acceleration screen
> → See ROI by vehicle stage

---

### 4. Upgrading Photos

*"This vehicle only has AI clone photos. Real ones would perform better."*

> **Who**: Dealer upgrading vehicle media
>
> ① See the performance gap
> → Clone vs Real photos compared side-by-side: clicks, leads, days on lot
>
> ② Hit "Upgrade Media" on the vehicle
>
> ③ Choose a method
> → Shoot with the mobile app, or upload existing photos
>
> ④ Follow the guided steps
>
> ⑤ Expected result: 18% faster turns, 24% more leads

---

### 5. Reviewing Financial Risk

*"How much capital do I have at risk and where is it sitting?"*

> **Who**: Dealer or Manager reviewing exposure
>
> ① Open Capital Exposure
> → Total exposure, 14-day burn projection, margin erosion rate
>
> ② Break it down by stage
> → How much capital is at risk in Watch vs Risk vs Critical
>
> ③ Break it down by source
> → Auction vs Trade-in vs Wholesale vs Direct
>
> ④ Break it down by price
> → Where are the big-ticket risks
>
> ⑤ Click any vehicle to take action

---

### 6. Fixing Website Conversion

*"Our website isn't converting. Where are we losing people?"*

> **Who**: Dealer optimizing their online presence
>
> ① Check the website score (0–100)
>
> ② See what's dragging it down
> → Media quality, photos per vehicle, page speed, form visibility, mobile experience
>
> ③ Follow the conversion funnel
> → Where visitors drop off: browsing → vehicle page → lead → appointment
>
> ④ Find the problem vehicles
> → Highlighted: high page views but low leads, or barely any visibility
>
> ⑤ See the revenue impact
> → Estimated annual revenue being missed from low conversion
>
> ⑥ Fix it → upgrade media or boost campaigns on underperformers

---

### 7. Working the To-Do Queue

*"I've got 30 minutes. What should I do to save the most margin?"*

> **Who**: Dealer with limited time
>
> ① Open Action Queue — everything ranked by dollar impact
>
> ② Filter by urgency — focus on "High" if time is tight
>
> ③ Work top to bottom
> → Each item: the vehicle, why it needs attention, expected dollar impact
>
> ④ Take action right there — campaign, media upgrade, or optimize
>
> ⑤ Need more context? Tap through to the full vehicle detail

---

### 8. Asking a Quick Question

*"I don't want to click around. Just tell me what's going on."*

> **Who**: Any user, any time
>
> ① Open AI Chat → see suggested questions to get started
>
> ② Type anything
> → "Which vehicles are at critical risk?"
> → "What's my total capital exposure?"
> → "Which vehicles need media upgrades?"
>
> ③ Get a clear, formatted answer
>
> ④ Follow the suggested follow-ups to keep exploring

---

### 9. Adding a New Vehicle

*"New vehicle just arrived. How do I get it into the system?"*

> **Who**: Dealer adding inventory
>
> ① Click "Add Vehicle" from the dashboard
>
> ② Enter the VIN
>
> ③ Choose how to get photos
> → 📱 App Capture — use the mobile app to photograph it
> → 📤 Upload — drag and drop existing photos
> → 🤖 AI Clone — instant AI-generated photos (fastest option)
>
> ④ Done — vehicle appears on the dashboard and starts tracking immediately

---

### 10. First Time Using the Dashboard

*"Dealer just signed up. What's their first experience?"*

> **Who**: New dealer, first login
>

>

> → What Velocity OS does, what it watches, what actions are available
>
> ③ See your top risk vehicles immediately
> → The system already knows what needs attention
>
> ④ Set up alerts
> → Choose email, SMS, or push notifications for margin warnings
>
> ⑤ Ongoing nudges
> → Tips like "87% of dealers who activated campaigns saw 23% faster turns"
